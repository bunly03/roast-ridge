Why we started Roast & Ridge

Roast & Ridge began as a class project exploring what it takes to bring a product online end-to-end: sourcing, presenting, and selling it through a real web storefront. We picked coffee because it's a product people already love shopping for — vivid origin stories, distinct flavor notes, and a purchase people happily repeat.

Every bag on this site is roasted in small batches and priced to reflect direct trade with growing regions across Africa and Latin America.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real, working store?

This is a demo storefront built to showcase a full shopping flow — product catalog, cart, and checkout. No real payments are processed. The checkout form is a simulation for demonstration purposes only.

Where does the product data come from?

Product details are stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) database and loaded live when the page opens.

How fresh is the coffee (in the demo story)?

In our fictional supply chain, beans ship within 48 hours of roasting.
